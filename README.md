# UBS AG Embraces Open Source

The [UBS AG](http://www.ubs.com/) GitHub organisation represents our firm’s commitment to giving back to the open source community at large, brings together the various projects that we contribute to and highlights some of our wider involvement across the industry.

## A very brief history of Open Source contributions at UBS

* In 2020 UBS joined [FINOS](https://finos.org/) as a Platinum member, the [Linux Foundation](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/) as a Silver member,
* 2020 also marked the first UBS fixes contributed to [Frameless](https://github.com/typelevel/frameless),
* As part of FINOS UBS helped lead the Open Source Readiness SIG and made FINOS project contributions including Vuu during the years which followed,
* UBS also joined the Green Software Foundation in 2022 as a steering member, contributing to their specifications

* UBS, in 2023, open-sourced the initial [sparkutils](https://github.com/sparkutils/) project, [Quality](https://sparkutils.github.io/quality/),
* In addition to our FINOS board member participation over the years we’re also active on FINOS’ [Technical Oversight Committee](https://github.com/finos/technical-oversight-committee),
* 2025 saw UBS continue its support of the OSS Community joining the [CNCF](https://www.cncf.io/) as a Silver member.

We have also significantly contributed over the years to [Scales XML](https://chris-twiner.github.io/scalesXml/), the wider [sparkutils](https://github.com/sparkutils/) organisation, several [terraform azure provider](https://github.com/hashicorp/terraform-provider-azurerm/) contributions, to [Apache Spark](https://spark.apache.org/) 4 and two new DMN projects as well.

### sparkutils

The [sparkutils organisation](https://github.com/sparkutils/) represents a collection of libraries for Apache Spark, including:

- [Quality](https://sparkutils.github.io/quality/) A high performance Data Quality and transformation library for Spark and Databricks runtimes using simple SQL snippets
- [Frameless](https://github.com/sparkutils/frameless) A fork of the excellent [Frameless](https://github.com/typelevel/frameless) library, providing wider version support for Spark 3.5, 4 and Databricks runtimes
- [Kogito-4-Spark](https://sparkutils.github.io/kogito-4-spark/) An initial version providing a Spark integration with the [Kogito](https://kogito.kie.org/) [DMN](https://www.omg.org/dmn/) engine – releasing soon.
